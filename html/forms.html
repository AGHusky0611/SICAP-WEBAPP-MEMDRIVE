Photography is capturing the moments that will last forever. An art that makes time stop and lets you see

would otherwise fade with time. Each photograph is a frozen moment in the continuous flow of life.

About Me

I'm a passionate photographer and computer science student with a unique perspective that combines
technical knowledge with artistic vision. My journey in photography began 5 years ago when I first
picked up a camera, and since then I've developed a style that focuses on portrait and street photography.

My technical background in computer science gives me an edge in post-processing and digital workflow,
allowing me to enhance images while maintaining their authentic feel. I believe that the best photographs
are those that tell stories and evoke emotions.

When I'm not coding or taking pictures, you can find me exploring new places, attending photography
workshops, or mentoring aspiring photographers in our community.
